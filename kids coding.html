Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mau Creative Hub all about?

We are a tech academy dedicated to helping individuals achieve their dreams by providing them with the skills they need to succeed in the ever-evolving tech industry.

What are the benefits of participating in your programs?

By participating in our programs, individuals can gain valuable skills and knowledge that are highly sought after in the tech industry. With hands-on experience and practical training, preparing participants for rewarding career opportunities in tech-related fields

What is the schedule for classes?

We offer flexible scheduling for classes. Classes are held three times weekly for adults and on weekends for kids, with each session lasting 2 hours. The frequency of classes depends on the choice of course

Do you offer online/virtual programs or classes?

Yes, we offer online/virtual classes in addition to in-person classes at our academy. Participants can choose the format that best suits their needs and preferences.

How can I enroll in your programs?

To enroll in our programs, simply follow these steps: 1. Select your choice of course from our course catalog. 2. Make a 50% payment to secure your enrollment. 3. Once payment is received, you will be emailed your class schedule, whether for offline or online classes.
